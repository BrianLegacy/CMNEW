1. Go to the M-PESA Menu
  2. Select Payment Services
  3. Select Pay Bill
  4. Enter 778827
  5. Enter as the account number
  6. Enter amount in KES
  7. Enter Your M-PESA Pin
  8. You will receive a notification from M-PESA with a confirmation code
  9. Then another notification from MSpace confirming the account top up
  10. Your MSpace account will be automatically topped up .
